Backend-specific functionality.

Note
Definitions from either vex::backend::opencl or vex::backend::cuda are directly brought into vex::backend namespace. Define either VEXCL_BACKEND_OPENCL or VEXCL_BACKEND_CUDA macro in order to select backend. You will also need to link to libOpenCL or libcuda accordingly.
